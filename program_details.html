Global Summit

ON Quality Higher Education

Sharing Values and Building Trust Beyond Borders

The global summit is expected to facilitate:

1) Sharing information and resources in higher education quality assurance.
2) Exchange and promotion of good practices
3) Sharing and creating value through power of alliance
4) Serving global student community by facilitating student mobility
5) Developing strategies for creating global resources in quality assurance

Expected Outcome:

Agreement or statement of intent among key global stakeholders of quality higher education to work together for common cause and explore untapped potential of networking among quality networks and quality assurance organisations.
