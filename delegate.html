Global Summit

ON Quality Higher Education

Sharing Values and Building Trust Beyond Borders

DELEGATES

The summit is targeted at global leaders in higher education quality assurance.
Senior policy makers from QA networks and QA bodies from selected countries, functionaries from national and state level higher education
ministries and regulatory bodies, experts and researchers in higher education, representatives from Industry, society, students and academia would be invited. About 150 delegates are expected to join the Global Quality Summit.

Key Speakers

Dr. Judith S. Eaton (USA)

President of Council for Higher Education Accreditation - (CHEA)

Formerly Chancellor of the Minnesota State Colleges and Universities, joined CHEA as President in August 1997. Judith has also been President of the Council for Aid to Education as well as Community College of Philadelphia and the Community College of Southern Nevada.

Prof. Dr. Jianxin Zhang (China)

President of Asia-Pacific Quality Network – (APQN)

Professor Dr. Jianxin Zhang is President of Asia Pacific Quality Network (APQN), the largest non-profit, non-government organization in the Asia-Pacific Region. She serves as Chief Expert of Yunnan Higher Education Evaluation Center (YHEEC), Director of Research Sector of Higher Education in the Research Institute of Higher Education in Yunnan University and Advisor of Council for CHEA (Higher Education Accreditation) International Quality Group (CIQG) in USA since 2012.

Rafael Llavori (Spain)

Nominee of European Network for Quality Assurance in Higher Education – (ENQA)

Head of Unit, Institutional and International Relations National Agency for Quality Assurance and Accreditation (ANECA).

Prof. M. Badr Aboul-Ela (UAE)

President of Arab Network for Quality Assurance in Higher Education (ANQAHE)

He was one of the founding members of the Commission for Academic Accreditation, in the UAE when established in 2000, and was appointed as its Director in February 2007. Dr. Aboul-Ela had significant and leading role in establishing the Arab Network for Quality Assurance in Higher Education (ANQAHE).

Dr. Jawaher Al Mudhaheki (Kingdom of Bahrain)

President - National Authority for Qualifications & Quality Assurance of Education and Training (QQA)

Chief Executive Officer, National Authority for Qualifications & Quality Assurance of Education & Training, (QQA). Also served as Board Director of INQAAHE and President of ANQAHE.

Ronny Heintze .M.A. (Germany)

Nominee of European Consortium of Accreditation – (ECA)

Senior-Referent / Senior-Consultant
Representative for International – Commissioner for International Affairs
AQAS e.V. - Agency for Quality Assurance through Accreditation of Study Programs.
